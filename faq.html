FAQ

How do I place an order?

Simply download our app, browse the menu, select your items, and proceed to checkout.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

Minimum order requirements vary by restaurant and may apply in some cases. Check the restaurant details for specific information.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you can track your order in real-time from the app. You'll receive updates on the status of your delivery.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, online payment gateways, and cash on delivery.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! You can customize your order according to your preferences. Simply add notes to your items during checkout.

What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies?

Many restaurants offer options for dietary restrictions or allergies. You can filter menu items based on your specific requirements or contact the restaurant directly for assistance.
